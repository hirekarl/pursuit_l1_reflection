# Case Study: BookBounty
## The Logistical Culling Consultant

### 🧐 The "Industrialist" Bet
**The Neglected Vertical:** People undergoing major life transitions (grief, moving, downsizing).
**The Friction:** Emotional and logistical "Culling Paralysis." Deciding which books to keep, sell, or donate when overwhelmed.
**Why Enterprise Fails Here:** eBay and Amazon focus on *buying*. They don't solve the *decision-making* friction of getting rid of things with sentimental value.

### ⚡ Builder Advocacy in Action
*   **Velocity:** Orchestrated multiple APIs (Open Library, eBay) to build a triage tool in 5 days.
*   **Sovereignty:** Applied the "Culling Goals" framework. The builder advocates for a psychological solution (Minimalist vs. Financial Optimizer) rather than just a technical one (API call).
*   **The Bargain:** A 5-day build that performs the work of a professional organizer and a book dealer combined.

### 📊 5-Day MVP Benchmarks
- **Day 1:** Multi-API Triage Logic (eBay/OpenLib).
- **Day 2:** Django + React Boilerplate and Authentication.
- **Day 3:** AI "Culling Consultant" (Gemini).
- **Day 4:** Search and Scanning UI.
- **Day 5:** Integration tests and deployment.

# Case Study: Kinetic
## SRE for the Self

### 🧐 The "Industrialist" Bet
**The Neglected Vertical:** High-performance engineers and builders at risk of burnout.
**The Friction:** The "Bio-Metric Blindness." Engineers have 100% observability on their code but 0% on their own sleep, stress, and relational health.
**Why Enterprise Fails Here:** Corporate wellness apps are often performative and focused on HR metrics rather than individual operational autonomy.

### ⚡ Builder Advocacy in Action
*   **Velocity:** Built an asynchronous, multi-agent triage system with FastAPI and SSE in 5 days.
*   **Sovereignty:** Rejected the "Dashboard" pattern in favor of a **Check-In Triage**. The builder advocates for *action* over *visualization*.
*   **The Bargain:** One natural-language check-in → three specialist agents → one prioritized triage list.

### 📊 5-Day MVP Benchmarks
- **Day 1:** FastAPI + asyncpg infrastructure.
- **Day 2:** Multi-Agent Triage Logic (Bio/Logistics/Relational).
- **Day 3:** SSE (Server-Sent Events) streaming implementation.
- **Day 4:** React + Tailwind mobile-first UI.
- **Day 5:** Burnout forecasting algorithm validation.

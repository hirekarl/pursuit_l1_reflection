## 🏗️ The L2 Shift: The "Factory Upgrade"
In L1, I proved that the **Individual Industrialist** can build. In L2, the goal is to prove that the Industrialist can **sustain and scale**. 

This document outlines the protocols, tools, and governance models I will use to navigate the next 8 weeks of the Pursuit AI-Native fellowship.

---

## 🔋 Pillar 1: Bio-Operational Stability (Powered by Kinetic)
To prevent the "Burnout Debt" that often follows a high-velocity sprint, I am implementing a **Kinetic-First Protocol**.

*   **Daily Triage:** Every morning starts with a natural-language check-in to the Kinetic engine. 
*   **The Constraint:** If Kinetic identifies a "Bio-Metric Red Zone" (Sleep < 6h, Relational Margin < 10%), L2 production is capped at 50% capacity to ensure architectural integrity.
*   **The Goal:** Moving from "High-Stress Sprints" to "Industrial Rhythm."

---

## 🤖 Pillar 2: Agentic Governance (The Staff 2.0)
I will expand my Multi-Agent Staff from "Task Runners" to "Autonomous Auditors."

*   **Aris (The Archivist):** Responsible for 100% documentation sync between `GEMINI.md`, `REFLECTION.md`, and code. No feature is "done" until Aris verifies the memory sync.
*   **Aura (The UI Auditor):** An L2-exclusive agent focused on the **Martha-First Protocol**. Aura runs automated accessibility simulations and "Dignity Audits" on every frontend component.
*   **Atlas 2.0 (The Architect):** Will now handle multi-repo orchestration, ensuring that common libraries (like my custom Django 6.x / React 19 templates) are synced across all L2 projects.

---

## 🛠️ Pillar 3: The L2 Technical Stack
We are doubling down on "Bleeding Edge Stability."

*   **Language/Env:** Python 3.13 (experimental features), `uv` for lightning-fast workspace management.
*   **AI Engine:** Gemini 2.5 Flash as the primary factory worker; Gemini 2.0 Pro for complex architectural migrations.
*   **Frontend:** React 19 with Server Components, prioritizing SSE (Server-Sent Events) for all AI interactions to maintain "Live Triage" aesthetics.
*   **Deployment:** Fully automated CI/CD via Render/GitHub Actions, with integrated `axe-core` accessibility checks.

---

## 🎯 Pillar 4: Dignity Milestones (The Market Bet)
My L2 projects will move deeper into the "Neglected Verticals."

1.  **Hyper-Local Resolution:** Moving from "NYC-wide" tools to "Block-level" tools.
2.  **Worker Autonomy:** Building tools that allow individual tradespeople (electricians, plumbers) to use AI for deterministic job scoping and safety triage.
3.  **The Feedback Loop:** Establishing a weekly "Human-in-the-Loop" session where I demo L2 progress to one "Martha" (a senior or non-technical worker) to ensure the tech remains grounded in reality.

---

## ⚡ The L2 Mantra
**"Speed is a gift, but Stability is the Moat."**

L1 was about proving I could build the engine. L2 is about building the infrastructure that allows the engine to run forever without breaking the builder.
